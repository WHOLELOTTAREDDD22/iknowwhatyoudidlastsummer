Overview
My main goal is providing a cheat sheet for the endless mode, including the recipes and attributes for all the special drinks and some (not all) of the non-special drinks, that fits roughly on one page without the need of scrolling through.

If any composition of customer wishes seems to be unavailable, just enter whatever and skip to the next one.

For me, this overview was very helpful, and upsetting a customer on purpose saved me some time, relative to scrolling a complete list of possible drinks. If you are looking for a complete overview, check out BOT MrPAPAPABLO's Guide.

A little tip to save some precious seconds: make sure that your cursor is on point (slightly to the right of the "Brew" prompt) and start clicking before the "Serve" prompt shows up. This way, your drink will be immediately served and you will have a couple of seconds more in the long run.
